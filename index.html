Our technology

High quality durable fabrics

Our signature fabric skins are made from durable polypropylene with a soft microfiber lining, and printed using the latest in sublimation technology. No adhesive, no adhesive residue, just high quality digital device protection.

Advanced superhydroboic technology

Our fabric features super-hydrophobic coatings that cause water to roll right off like magic. We called it FabrificDry™, a technology that we have developed in-house after extensive R&D. This fabric lines are expert manufactured with our proprietary nanoparticle technology, making them shower-proof, stain-proof, waterproof and self cleaning.

Beautiful Designs

Fabrific™ features innovative fabric designs made to help you create beautiful, unique look for your digital devices. Our fabrics are specially conceived by top designers and made with care by our craftsmen in Italy.
